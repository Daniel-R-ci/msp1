About us

We are not a formal club or organisation, just a group of people interestid in model railroading, primarily interested in model railroading i N-scale (1:160) and meeting others who share that interest.

There is no need for you to already have one or more modules to join us. The interest and willingness to join one of the more social ways on ejoying model railroading. We don't have any membership articles or fees.

Our primary form of internal communication is through an email group where we announce activites and share information of interest.

We also have pages on Facebook and Youtube where you can see what happens at some of our activites.
